---
title: Why the fixture stays generic
description: A note on why the example site is intentionally documentation-first instead of project-specific.
date: 2026-04-05
status: live
tags:
  - fixture
  - docs
summary: A generic fixture ages better, teaches more clearly, and catches regressions without tying the package to one external project story.
---

The fixture works best when it explains TreeSeed itself. Once the example site becomes too attached to one specific project, it starts teaching the wrong thing: that TreeSeed only makes sense in one narrative frame.
