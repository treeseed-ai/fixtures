This page is the bridge into the TreeSeed books and docs.

Use the [Architecture book](/knowledge/architecture/) to understand the high-level runtime and content surfaces. Use the [Developer book](/knowledge/developer/) to understand local workflow and authoring. Use the [Operations book](/knowledge/operations/) to understand verification and deploy-facing concerns.

The important architectural idea in the fixture is not scale. It is legibility. The tenant should make it obvious how pages, docs, books, questions, objectives, people, agents, and forms fit together.
